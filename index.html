RU who
  • RUwho makes it easier to search the Rutgers database and reveal what info of yours Rutgers is making public.
  • This service is not affiliated with Rutgers. Data is fetched from the public RU DB and is relied upon for accuracy.
  • Pressing enter or submitting the form to search is not needed; lookup will passively begin as a query is typed.
  • Filter results with the "role:{role}" search command. Accepted arguments: "student", "faculty", "staff", "guest".
Sensitive contact information (emails, phone numbers, etc.) will not be shown for privacy as you're not connected to RUWireless.
